My Background and Football's Impact

My name is Parker Jenkins and ever since my best friend in kindergarten introduced me to the sport of football, I have been a diehard follower of all that happens in league. My friend's font-family is originally from Wisconsin, which means that they are Packers fans, and because I was always was at their house, I became one too. Though following one team was not enough for me as I wanted to keep track of all the teams while feeling emersed in the sport.

Fantasy Football gives live updates as the games are happening all while also calculating the stats for the point system. The points work differently according to how each league sets them, but they follow the amount of yards a player is either able to gain on the run , or throw if they are a quarterback. Touchdowns give a lot of points to players though quarterbacks get a little less since they are normally throwing them in. However Qb's do not get credit if they hand the ball off for a run. Defenses gain points by positive plays for them but lose some if they are scored on and kickers only get points if they make it.

Season

Games starting from the beginning of the season to the End

The season begins around the month of September before the start of the actual League season, giving people time to prepare and get the best possible team assembled before Week 1. In a regular NFL season there are 17 weeks, which means for fantasy football, that there are 14 weeks and the last 3 are for playoffs. Every Wednesday players can try to pick up free agents, available players, if they had not already put in a bid for them. Each week officially ends after the last game is played that week, which is normally on a Monday. Players have to be aware of the different game times, in case they acciedentaly forget to make roster changes. Kickoff of a game makes it official and you can not move a player thats playing or played until the end of the week.
